About Me

Pranav Rajan is a senior at Lincoln East High School, where he has participated in debate, Science Olympiad, Varsity football, and research at NASA SEES. Academically, Pranav enjoys studying computer science and educational policy.

Pranav has been responsible for implementing the ideas behind CodeForChange. Since 2017, he has met with organizations, companies, educators, faculty, school districts, administrators, and students to develop the infrastructure behind CodeForChange.


Pranav now serves as the executive director of CodeForChange and continues to run summer and academic year projects/programs for students. His goal is to spread the opportunity of CodeForChange throughout the Midwest, while collaborating with other students interested in similar causes.

My Future

My goal is to better science education for students through creating interdisciplinary opportunities between STEM fields and Business/Literature field.

To do this, I am seeking a degree in Computer Science and Education.

From there, I would like to work for an institution that shares similar ideas for education reform as me.
